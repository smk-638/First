About Us

SMK Builders has been pioneering the growth of TN since 2004 with unmatched quality construction. Over 100 completed projects and several ongoing projects, we have something to offer for every need.

At SMK Builders the entire focus is on the customer. We have been redefining living spaces keeping the changing needs of our clients in mind. Our locations are always prime and we ensure that each of our projects is a landmark in that area.

We believe that the real estate sector in India will witness major changes in favour of the customer; and we are geared up to that already. Transparency is our forte. We sell all flats on full cheque terms only. We sell on carpet area basis and offer a unique 5-year warranty on homes.

We recognize that in our country, home is the realization of a dream. We are proud to have helped hundreds of families realize this dream with satisfaction. This trust is our most valuable asset and our guiding philosophy. So if you are looking for high quality premises with peace for mind complimentary, Welcome to SMK Builders.
